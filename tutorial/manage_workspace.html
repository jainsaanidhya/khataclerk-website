Working Inside Your Workspace

Once your workspace is set up, this is your day-to-day workflow — upload invoices, review the AI-extracted data, and push everything to Tally in a few clicks.

Step 1

Your First Upload

Upload your purchase or sales invoices to your workspace. KhataClerk will automatically extract all the key fields using AI.

On the Upload page, select your invoice type — Purchase or Sales — using the toggle at the top of the upload panel.

Click "Choose Files" and select your invoice(s) from your device. You can select a single file or multiple files at the same time. Supported formats: PDF, PNG, JPG, TIFF.

Click "Upload" to send your files to the server. Once the upload completes, click "Start Processing" to trigger the AI extraction engine.

Once processing is complete, click the "Review" button next to any file to open it directly, or navigate to Document Review to see the full list of all your uploaded documents.

Step 2

Document Review

Here you will see all the invoices you have uploaded. Review the AI-extracted data, approve correct ones, and manage or delete any that need attention.

1

View Your Invoice List

Navigate to Document Review. You will see all uploaded invoices with their AI extraction status.

● Ready for Tally review ● Review needed
2

Actions Per Invoice

Each invoice in the list has the following action buttons:

Open

Opens the invoice in detail view so you can see and edit the AI-extracted data fields.

Tally review

Moves the invoice directly to the Tally Review tab for final check and push.

Approve for Tally

If the invoice data is extracted correctly, this approves it and moves it to Tally Review in one click.

Delete

Permanently removes the invoice from your workspace.

Step 3

Tally Review

All documents you approved from Document Review will appear here. This is your final checkpoint before posting to Tally.

⚠️ Before you begin: Make sure your Tally Bridge is active and Tally is open on your system. → How to configure Tally & Tally Bridge
1

View Your Approved Invoices

Navigate to Tally Review. Invoices come here after being approved in Document Review, each with a status:

● Ready to push to Tally ● Review required ● Pushed to Tally
2

Fix "Review Required" Invoices

For invoices showing Review required, click Open to view and correct any errors. Once saved, the status updates automatically to Ready to push to Tally.

3

Push to Tally

Select all invoices marked Ready to push to Tally using the checkboxes, then click "Push to Tally". All selected invoices will be posted as individual vouchers in Tally — perfectly mapped and ready for audit.

✅ Tip: Use the Select All checkbox to quickly select every "Ready to push" invoice and push them all in one batch.
