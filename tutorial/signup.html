How to signup

1

Navigate to the Portal

Go to the "Login or Signup" portal.

[ GIF: Clicking Login or Signup Button ]
2

Click 'Sign up'

Since you are a new user, look for the text "Don't have an account?" at the bottom of the form and click on the "Sign up" link.

[ GIF: Clicking 'Sign up' at the bottom of the form ]
3

Fill in Your Details

Enter your Full Name, your Email, and choose a strong Password.

  • At least 8 characters
  • One lowercase + uppercase letter
  • One number
  • One special character
[ GIF: Entering Name, Email, and Password ]
4

Create Account

Click the "Create Account" button. You will then receive a verification email to activate your account and start automating your invoices.

[ GIF: Clicking Create Account button ]
5

Verify Your Email

Open your email inbox and look for a message from KhataClerk. Click the confirmation link inside the mail to verify your identity and activate your account.

[ GIF: Opening Email & Clicking Link ]
