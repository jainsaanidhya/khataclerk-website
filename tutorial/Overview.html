Getting Started with KhataClerk

Welcome to KhataClerk! This guide will help you set up your account, manage your organization, and configure your workspaces for seamless invoice automation.

What is KhataClerk?

KhataClerk is an intelligent invoice automation platform. We provide a structured way to manage your business through a dedicated Organization and flexible Workspaces, ensuring your accounting data is always organized and secure.

  • Organization-Level Control: Manage team roles, admins, and invites.
  • Account Transparency: Real-time tracking of page usage and plan limits.
  • Workspace Isolation: Keep different departments or clients separated within your organization.

The 3-Step Setup

  • 1 Getting Started: Create your account and verify your email.
  • 2 Organizations: Manage your team roles and monitor your plan usage.
  • 3 Workspaces: Create or join dedicated spaces for clients and departments to keep your bookkeeping organized.
