Create & Join Workspaces

Workspaces are isolated environments where you process invoices. You can have separate workspaces for different clients, departments, or financial years.

1

Joining an existing Workspace

Once you have the invite code,Access the "Join Workspace" card from your home dashboard enter the code to join the workspace. Once joined, it will instantly reflect in your dashboard.

[ GIF: Joining a Workspace via Invite Code ]
2

Creating a new Workspace

Access the "Create Workspace" card from the Quick Actions section on your home dashboard. Then, enter your Workspace Name and GSTIN in the modal to create your new workspace instantly.
