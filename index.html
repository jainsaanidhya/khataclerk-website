Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is KhataClerk?
We guarantee 95% accuracy from day one. Our system improves as it learns your vendors, approaching 99% accuracy over time.
What accounting software are supported?
Direct integration with Tally (ERP 9, Prime, Cloud), Zoho Books, QuickBooks Online, and Busy. Plus REST API for custom integrations.
Can multiple users post to one Tally?
Yes! Unlimited users can scan and process invoices from anywhere. All entries sync to your central Tally server in real-time.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption, store data in Indian data centers, and are SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data never leaves India.
What file formats work?
PDF, JPG, PNG, TIFF, multi-page scans. Even handwritten invoices and low-quality photos work perfectly.
Can I customize field extraction?
Yes! Just tell us what fields you need extracted. We configure everything so you don't touch any settings.
What if the AI makes a mistake?
You review everything before posting. Edit any field with one click. The system learns from your corrections.
Is there a contract?
No lock-in. Monthly billing. Cancel anytime. We earn your business every month.
How fast is setup?
Sign up Upload test invoice See results. Takes 5 minutes. Full integration setup in 30 minutes with our team.
Do you support regional languages?
Yes! Our system handles invoices in English, Hindi, and major regional languages commonly used in Indian business documents.
